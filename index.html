About CCE

COMMUNITY COWORKING ENVIRONMENT (CCE) is a start-up that thinks that efficient working is about working smarter, thus increasing productivity. It’s important for a work place to help individuals to be more effective, creative and collaborative, something that many can’t achieve specially with the outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic, that is causing people to work from home in a less ideal conditions. That is where CCE comes in to help increase not only the correlation between productivity and employee satisfaction, but also to create an environment for a maximum working result.

OUR MISSION

We are dedicated to bring and facilitate access to a friendly and low cost working environment to individuals, start-ups, and small businesses, a place that helps you explore the creative side of all business minded people.
